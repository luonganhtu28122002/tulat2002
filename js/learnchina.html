Learn to write kanji calligraphy and 3 notes students need to know

Learning to write Chinese calligraphy is learning a very old script in the world. And kanji calligraphy is an art in beautiful Chinese writing. If you want to learn how to write Chinese calligraphy, which method should you use? We will learn about how to learn simple calligraphy through the following article.

Learn to hold a pen

To write beautiful kanji calligraphy and write it properly. First of all, kanji calligraphy enthusiasts must know how to hold a brush correctly. The way you hold the pen doesn't seem to matter. But it's actually the first step to pay attention to. It has a great influence on handwriting and later writing practice.

When writing, to hold the brush correctly is to hold it so that the palm is empty. Our fingers when writing can flexibly control the pen handle when writing. If you are a beginner to practice writing Chinese calligraphy. Then hold the pen close to the bristles of the pen. The distance from the feather to the ring finger is about 3cm.

The appropriate pen holding position when writing is the student's right hand holding the pen. Place your left hand on the table and place it across your chest. When the correct way of holding the pen is established, the correct writing posture is established. That is also a way to increase the efficiency of forging beautiful calligraphy.

When learning to write basic strokes, it can be quite monotonous and sometimes frustrating. Therefore, students are required to be diligent and persistent in pursuing them. Every day, no matter how much or how little, you should practice regularly. When the basic strokes you write have mastered. Then practice and write Chinese calligraphy will become easier, writing less mistakes.

Learn Chinese calligraphy for beginners. After the process has mastered the basic strokes already. Then the next step will be to practice writing according to the strokes written by the ancients. Writing and learning to write calligraphy is similar to arranging furniture. Students must find a way to display as well as arrange the strokes for the most reasonable. Although everyone's aesthetic opinion may be different. However, Chinese calligraphy will have certain writing rules. When you have practiced handwriting according to the ancient writing style. At the same time, you also understand the necessary writing principles. Now you can completely combine those writing principles. Plus the individual's own aesthetic. To create your own brand and style of calligraphy!
